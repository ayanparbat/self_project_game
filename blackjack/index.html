How to Play
  • The goal of blackjack is to beat the dealer's hand without going over 21
  • Face cards (K, Q, J) and 10 are worth 10 points. Aces (A) are worth 1 or 11, whichever makes a better hand
  • Game starts with two cards, one of the dealer's cards is hidden until the end
  • To 'Hit' is to ask for another card. To 'Stand' is to end your turn
  • If you go over 21 you bust, and the dealer wins regardless of the dealer's hand
  • If you are dealt 21 from the start (Ace & 10 points card), you got a blackjack
  • Dealer will hit until his/her cards total 17 or higher
  • Deck is a standard 52 playing cards (excluding Joker)
  • Final serving card set is made by the shuffled multiple decks (default = 4decks). ei, when you start the game the serving container holds 52x4=208 shuffled cards which can be played upto 65% of the total cards untill auto shuffle
  • You can shuffle and the reset the serving container by hitting shuffle button
